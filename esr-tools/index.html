DoubleEclipse's Eastern Sun Resurrected Tools

Eastern Sun Resurrected is a mod for Diablo 2: Resurrected

Item Database

Combines most item related pages from the Official ESR site into one huge, searchable page. Reads data live from the ESR site.

Life Calculator

Calculates your character's total Life based on stats, skills and items. ESR version dependent.

Cube Recipes Search

Quickly look up recipes for the Horadric Cube to craft, upgrade, and transmute your gear. Reads data live from the ESR site.

Area Levels

Shows all game area levels. Includes Special Areas and Endgame maps. ESR version dependent.

Materials Finder

Search who and where drops Organs and some cubing materials. ESR version dependent.
